Who Are We?

Vision:

To create large level metaverse games which benefit both Gamers & Businesses with values & entertainment. We aim to create a large number of employments via Gaming.

Problem:

Limited opportunity and the gap between businesses, gamers/users & company. We are committed to reduce the gap and solve the issue without charging the gamers a penny.

We Are:

Team of student innovators/entrepreneurs, who created two PC games that got 10 Million+ downloads & received mass media coverage without any investment, mentorship, technical support or experience.

Creating Dreams:

Forget pay-to-win. GauravGo Games flips the script by rewarding players for their time. They integrate brand advertisements to create a win-win: players earn, brands connect.
