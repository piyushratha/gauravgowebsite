Privacy Policy

GauravGo Games Technologies Private Limited

Welcome to GauravGo Games Technologies Private Limited, a premier gaming startup dedicated to crafting immersive experiences inspired by real stories and cinematic scenarios through cutting-edge technology. This Privacy Policy elucidates our practices for collecting, processing, and safeguarding your personal data, reflecting our commitment to fostering a secure and trusted community of gamers while positioning our state as a global gaming hub.

Visit our website: https://gauravgo.com

1. Comments and User Data

When visitors submit comments, we collect the data provided in the comment form, including the visitor’s IP address and browser user agent, to aid in spam detection. Upon approval, your profile picture may be displayed alongside your comment.

2. Media

To protect your privacy, please refrain from uploading images containing EXIF GPS location data. Visitors may download images from our site and extract any embedded location information.

3. Cookies

  • Comments: If you opt to save your name, email, and website in cookies when leaving a comment, these will persist for one year for your convenience.
  • Login: A temporary cookie checks browser compatibility upon visiting the login page and is deleted upon closing. Post-login, cookies store your login details and display preferences for two days, with display settings lasting one year. Selecting “Remember Me” extends login cookies to two weeks.
  • Content Editing: Editing or publishing content triggers a cookie to track the post ID, expiring after one day.

4. Embedded Content

Embedded content (e.g., videos or articles) from third-party sites may function as if you visited those sites directly, potentially allowing data collection, cookie usage, and interaction tracking by the third party.

5. Data Sharing

For security, your IP address is included in password reset emails if you request one.

6. Data Retention

Comments and metadata are retained indefinitely to facilitate future approvals. For registered users, profile data is stored and can be viewed, edited, or deleted (excluding usernames) at any time, with administrators retaining full edit access.

7. User Data Rights

If you hold an account or have commented, you may request an export of your personal data or request its deletion, subject to exceptions for administrative, legal, or security purposes.

8. Data Transmission

Visitor comments may be screened using automated spam detection services.

9. Games Download

  • Games on our website are free unless otherwise specified, with all intellectual property rights reserved by GauravGo Games Technologies Private Limited. While inspired by real events, game narratives are fictional, created for entertainment, with no intent to resemble actual persons or offend.
  • We uphold the highest data protection standards, regularly updating our practices to align with global regulations. Your data is securely managed to enhance your gaming experience, and we welcome any privacy concerns you may have.
  • This policy underscores our commitment to data privacy, ensuring secure handling of personal information (e.g., name, email, IP address) in compliance with international standards, providing a seamless and confidential gaming experience.

10. Contact Information for Data Protection Inquiries

Email: contact@gauravgo.com

Phone: +91 7352279074

We are dedicated to safeguarding your personal data and respecting your rights. Please contact us for any inquiries regarding data protection, access, rectification, or deletion.
